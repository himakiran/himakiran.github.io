CADETS TRAINING WING

Cadets Training Wing (CTW), MCTE was raised in Jul 2000 as per Army HQ letter No 08044/10+2/Org 2(MP)/(A)/4136 dt Oct 98. Two more CTWs namely CTW, CME, Pune and CTW, MCEME, Secunderabad were also raised simultaneously. These CTWs have been raised to train Gentlemen Cadets (GCS) of 10+2 Technical Entry Scheme (TES). CTW, MCTE started functioning from temp premises consisting of 07 barracks allotted in Air Field Area. The motto of CTW is 'POWER THROUGH KNOWLEDGE'. MCTE crest was adopted till Autumn Term 2004. However, wef Spring Term 2005, new crest of CTW has been adopted. The crest of the Cadets training wing is composed of the sword of the profession of arms, crossed with a flash of lightening, symbolizing power superimposed in the middle by the 'DHARMA CHAKRA'. The scroll bears the inscription "POWER THROUGH KNOWLEDGE"

FACULTY OF COMBAT COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONIC WARFARE

The fac was raised in 1946 as No 1 Wg of the erstwhile School of Signals to impart tac trg. The first Officer to comd No 1 Wg was Maj (later Brig) Apar Singh, MBE. Later in Jan 1949, it came to be known as Tac Wg and Maj KD Bhasin was the first Offr Comdg. On 01 Oct 1967, the School of Sigs was re-designated as Military College of Telecommunication Engineering and the Wgs were renamed as fac. The Tac Wg came to be known as Fac of Combat Comns (FCC) and Lt Col (Later Maj Gen) Suljit Singh became the first Fac Cdr.In Jan 1979, the Electronic Warfare (EW) Wg was added to the fac and the fac was re-designated as Fac of Combat Comns and Electronic Warfare (FCC & EW). Since EW was considered integral to combat comns, the word “Electronic Warfare” was later dropped from the name of the Fac. In Jun 2012, the Fac was re-named as FCC & EW and has been nominated as the “ Centre of Excellence for Electronic Warfare”.

FACULTY OF COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

Commencing under the unassuming name of No 2 Sqn in Oct 46, it had wrls and line tps in addn to signal tactics and trial demo tps. The Sqn was first commanded by Maj Baker. In 1947, the Sqn became No 2 Coy and conducted the first Offrs Short Telecom Course, the fore-runner of today’s graduate and post-graduate courses. In 1949, the Company became No 2 Wg comprising wrls, line, wksp, tech maint and ciph coys and in 1951 the rk of the Cdr was upgraded to Lt Col. The next reorg took place in 1967 when, the present Fac came into being.Beginning with a ltd number of courses in 1947, such as Offrs Long Telecom and F of S Courses, it grew steadily to be responsible for Offrs Long Telecomm, Offrs Short Telecom, Offrs Adv Line, Offrs Advance Wireless, F of S and Cl I trg of wksp cats in 1950. In 1962 and 1965, the Fac undertook the tech trg needs of the units necessitated by the expansion in the Army.
